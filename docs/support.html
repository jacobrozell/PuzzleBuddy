Puzzle Buddy

Getting help

Open an issue on GitHub or contact the developer through your usual support channel.

Common topics

  • Version 1.0: Your collection stays on your device. No account is required.
  • Pick next puzzle: Tap the dice icon on the puzzle list to randomly choose from your To-Do backlog (optional tag and piece-count filters).
  • Backup: Import and export arrive in a future update. Version 1.0 keeps your collection on device only.
  • Duplicate check: Tap the barcode icon on the puzzle list to scan a box and see if you already own it — works offline.
  • Barcode lookup: Optional. Settings → Barcode & cataloging → Look up product from barcode sends digits to UPCitemdb only when enabled.
  • Photos: Puzzle cover images are stored on your device with each entry.
  • Account sync: Planned for a future update. Sign-in and cloud backup are not available in version 1.0.
