Privacy Policy

Puzzle Buddy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Puzzle Buddy stores your puzzle collection on your device. We do not sell your data.

When account sign-in is available in a future update, optional cloud backup may use Firebase (Firestore) associated with your account. Version 1.0 does not require an account.

Data we collect

  • Puzzle metadata you enter (title, piece count, ratings, photos) — stored on your device in version 1.0
  • Anonymous usage analytics and crash reports (no email in analytics events)
  • Account email and display name — only if and when you choose to create an account in a future release

Optional barcode lookup

If you turn on Look up product from barcode in Settings, Puzzle Buddy sends the barcode digits to UPCitemdb to suggest a product title or brand. This happens only when you scan or add a puzzle and only after checking your saved collection on device. We do not send your name, email, or puzzle photos. You can leave this off and use the app fully offline except for that optional feature.
