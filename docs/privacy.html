Privacy Policy

Puzzle Buddy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Puzzle Buddy stores your puzzle collection on your device. We do not sell your data. No account is required.

Data we collect

  • Puzzle metadata you enter (title, piece count, ratings, photos) — stored on your device
  • Anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Analytics) and crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics). Events are allowlisted and do not include puzzle titles or photos.

Analytics and crash reporting

Version 1.0 may send anonymous app-open and puzzle-count events to Firebase Analytics, and crash diagnostics to Firebase Crashlytics, to improve stability. You can review Firebase’s policies at firebase.google.com/support/privacy. Puzzle Buddy does not use advertising tracking or sell your data.

Barcode scanning

Barcode duplicate-check and quick-add use only data stored on your device. Puzzle Buddy does not send barcode digits to third-party product databases.
